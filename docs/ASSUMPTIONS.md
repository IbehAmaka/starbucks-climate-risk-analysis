# Key Assumptions

## Climate Data
- CMIP6 multi-model ensemble (CanESM5, CNRM-CM6-1, MIROC6)
- Temperature units: Kelvin (converted to Celsius)
- Precipitation units: kg/m²/s (converted to mm/year)

## Coffee Optimal Conditions
- Temperature range: 15-24°C
- Optimal temperature: 19.5°C
- Precipitation range: 1,200-2,000mm/year
- Optimal precipitation: 1,600mm/year

## Elevation-Dependent Offsets
- Low elevation (<900m): 9°C daily range
- Mid elevation (900-1,500m): 7-8°C daily range
- High elevation (>1,500m): 5-6°C daily range

## Financial Parameters
- Price elasticity: -0.8 (industry standard)
- Discount rate: 8% (corporate standard)
- Analysis horizon: 2025-2050 (25 years)
- Base coffee price: $3.50/kg
- Revenue per kg: $73.50/kg

## Limitations
- Analysis assumes fixed sourcing regions
- No farmer adaptation scenarios
- Linear stress-to-yield relationships
- No technological breakthroughs assumed

## Adaptation parameters
Base Case (0.4): Middle of empirical range (35-50%)
Conservative (0.2): Lower bound to show pessimistic case
Optimistic (0.6): Upper bound to show best-case adaptation
